A Few Tips To Get Started

Do Your Own Research

This website will give you many tools to get started with PC Building. There are useful tips in the PCPARTPICKING and PHYSICALBUILD sections, as well as a list of useful links and section to ask questions in the submit a question section. However, there are so many ways to build a pc and the neat thing about building your own pc is that it can be tailored to suit your own needs. Make sure to use our resources and others as you put together your pc.

Evolving Website

PCBuilder is a website that will evolve and change over time. The tech world is an ever-changing space and so the opinions we express today on which components to buy may not be the same in 6 months or in a year. Be sure to check back before you make your final build for the latest information.

Don't Rush

Many people wait until the last minute to say, "Now I am ready to build a pc" without taking the steps needed to learn pc building. You will be able to put together a much better build if you do your own research ahead of time. It is also more cost effective if you can look for deals ahead of time.

You will learn as you build

Building a pc is a learning experience. When you build your first pc, you won't know everything. It is ok to make a few mistakes along the way.

Don't Fear

Don't get too stressed out about it. You are building a cool piece of technology for yourself and more than likely you will be extremely satisfied with the final product. Although, you might be worried about it, you will be fine. PC Building is getting easier and easier and there are more resources available than ever before.
